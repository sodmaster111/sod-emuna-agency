# Uptime Kuma Monitors

Configure the following monitors in Uptime Kuma for Coolify deployments:

- **Frontend** – `https://www.sodmaster.online` — interval: 1 minute.
- **Backend Health** – `https://api.sodmaster.online/health` — interval: 30 seconds.
- **Backend Deep Health** – `https://api.sodmaster.online/health/deep` — interval: 5 minutes.
- **Telegram Gateway** – `https://tg.sodmaster.online/api/status` — interval: 1 minute.
- **WhatsApp Gateway** – `https://wa.sodmaster.online/api/status` — interval: 1 minute.
- **TON Service** – `https://ton.sodmaster.online/api/status` — interval: 1 minute.
